Trusted Collaborations, Exceptional Results.

People and companies we have worked with

Indian Naval Academy, Ezhimala: Empowering Excellence with Quality Supplies

We proudly supported the Indian Naval Academy by providing essential supplies, including fibre boats and hospital items. Our commitment to quality ensures the Academy continues to excel in training and service.

Sarada Krishna Homoeopathic Medical College, Kanyakumari: Advancing Education with Innovative Solutions

We contributed to the growth of Sarada Krishna Homoeopathic Medical College by supplying state-of-the-art multifunctional manikin models. These tools enhance the educational experience and equip students for real-world medical practice.

Local Self-Government Department, Kerala: Supporting Every Level of Governance

We are proud to supply a wide array of products to the Local Self-Government Department, serving all levels of governance, including Panchayats, municipalities, and district offices across Kerala.

Anganwadi Centers, Kerala: Inspiring Learning through Play

We’ve supplied a wide range of toys to Anganwadi centers across Kerala, enhancing early childhood education. Our products support a nurturing and playful environment for young learners.

Agricultural Office, Kerala: Equipping the Future of Agriculture

From machinery to essential tools, we’ve supplied a full range of agricultural equipment to support the Agricultural Office in Kerala. Our partnership ensures that modern solutions drive the future of farming.
